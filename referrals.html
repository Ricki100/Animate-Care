Suitability

Who we support

Animate Care considers referrals for young people who need therapeutic residential care, emotional containment and consistent adult support.

  • Children and young people aged 8-17.
  • Emotional and behavioural difficulties.
  • Trauma histories or disrupted attachments.
  • Need for PACE-informed therapeutic support.
  • Planned or emergency residential placement needs.
  • Support under spot purchase or framework arrangements.

Admissions

Our referral and admissions process

Initial enquiry

Call or email to discuss the child, current risks, availability and urgency.

Referral documents

Send care plans, risk assessments, chronology, education information and health details.

Manager review

The Registered Manager reviews suitability, impact on existing residents and staffing requirements.

Professional discussion

We speak with social workers and relevant professionals to clarify needs and expectations.

Visit or transition

Where possible, we support a planned visit and transition before admission.

Placement decision

A decision is made only where we can provide a safe, suitable environment.

Checklist

Referral documentation

For the fastest assessment, please include current and historical documents where available.

  • Referral form and reason for placement.
  • Care plan, placement plan and chronology.
  • Risk assessments and behaviour support plans.
  • Education documents, including EHCP, IEP or PEP.
  • Health, CAMHS and medication information.
  • Family contact arrangements and legal status.

For urgent placements, please call directly

Email is useful for documents, but urgent referral decisions should start with a phone call.

Referrals: questions answered

How do I make a referral to Animate Care?

Call 0118 440 0118 or email info@animatecare.co.uk with referral documentation. Our Registered Manager reviews all referrals personally.

Does Animate Care accept emergency placements?

Yes. We consider planned and emergency admissions, subject to suitability, safety and impact on existing residents.

What age range does Animate Care accept?

Animate Care supports young people aged 8 to 17.

Who can refer a child?

Referrals usually come from local authority professionals, social workers or commissioners.
