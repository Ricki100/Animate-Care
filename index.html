What makes us different

Care that goes beyond a roof over their head

Every child deserves more than a place to stay. At Animate Care, we combine safe accommodation with a whole-person therapeutic approach grounded in PACE: Playfulness, Acceptance, Curiosity and Empathy.

Our team works with each child, their family and placing authority to build an individualised care plan. We do not treat children as cases to be managed. We treat them as young people with unique stories, real potential and every right to a better future.

Your questions, answered

What age group does Animate Care support?

Animate Care provides residential care for children and young people aged 8 to 17 who have emotional and behavioural difficulties.

Is Animate Care Ofsted registered?

Yes. Animate Care is registered with Ofsted and operates in full compliance with the Children's Homes (England) Regulations 2015, the Care Standards Act 2000, and the Children's Acts 1989 and 2004.

What is the staff ratio at Animate Care?

Animate Care maintains a minimum 1:1 staffing ratio, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including waking night staff.

What therapeutic approach does Animate Care use?

Our care is built on the PACE model: Playfulness, Acceptance, Curiosity and Empathy. It helps children feel safe enough to engage, connect and grow.

How do I refer a child to Animate Care?

Referrals come from local authority professionals under spot purchase or framework agreements. Call 0118 440 0118 or email info@animatecare.co.uk to discuss availability and suitability.
